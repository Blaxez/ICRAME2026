About the Conference

ICRAME 2026 is a global forum bringing together researchers, academicians, innovators, and industry experts to discuss cutting-edge developments across engineering disciplines.

The conference promotes cross-domain collaboration, emphasizing the convergence of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture.

  • Proceedings published with Taylor & Francis (Routledge)
  • Double-blind peer review with mandatory Turnitin check (<15%)
  • Post-conference webinars and global visibility programs

Review Process

Our rigorous double-blind peer-review process ensures the highest quality of research and integrity.

STEP 01

Submission Check

Manuscripts are visually screened for completeness, formatting compliance, and scope relevance. Plagiarism is verified using Turnitin.

Conducted by the Editorial Board
STEP 02

Double-Blind Review

Two or more independent experts evaluate the work on originality, technical depth, and methodology. Identities remain anonymous.

Assigned by Program Committee
STEP 03

Evaluation & Decision

Authors revise based on feedback. Final decisions (Accept/Reject) are made by Chairs based on the reviews and revision quality.

Decision by Program Chairs
STEP 04

Publication

Accepted and presented papers are forwarded for publication. Ethical violations lead to immediate rejection.

Ethics & Policies

The conference is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity. All stakeholders are expected to follow these guidelines.

Authors

  • Submit original, unpublished work.
  • Properly cite sources; avoid plagiarism.
  • Disclose conflicts of interest.
  • Ensure all authors contributed.

Reviewers

  • Maintain confidentiality.
  • Provide objective feedback.
  • Declare conflicts of interest.
  • Avoid using unpublished material.

Editors

  • Ensure fair, timely review.
  • Decide based on academic merit.
  • Maintain confidentiality.
  • Act on misconduct.
