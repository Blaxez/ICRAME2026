About ICRAME 2026

Fostering innovation and collaboration at the intersection of multidisciplinary engineering.

About the Conference

The International Conference on Recent Advances in Multidisciplinary Engineering (ICRAME 2026) is an academic initiative aimed at providing a common platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to share recent developments, innovative ideas, and research findings across multiple engineering and technology domains. The conference will include keynote lectures by eminent experts, peer-reviewed technical paper presentations, panel discussions, and student-centric academic activities. The conference proceedings have been accepted by Taylor & Francis (Routledge) for publication with ISBN and DOI, and will be submitted for Scopus indexing as per publisher policy.

Objectives

  • To provide an international platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to present and exchange recent advances in multidisciplinary engineering and technology.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and innovation addressing key global challenges such as sustainability, smart infrastructure, healthcare technologies, and digital transformation.
  • To align engineering research with national priorities of India, including Make in India, Digital India, Atmanirbhar Bharat, Industry 4.0, and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • To encourage collaboration between academia, industry, and research organizations at national and international levels for translating research outcomes into real-world applications.
  • To provide a high-quality forum for young researchers, Ph.D. scholars, and students to enhance research skills, publication quality, and global visibility.
  • To disseminate cutting-edge research outcomes through peer-reviewed publications, keynote lectures, and technical sessions, contributing to global knowledge advancement.
  • To foster ethical, inclusive, and responsible engineering practices that support sustainable socio-economic development at national and global levels.

Benefits to Students, Researchers, and Institutions

The proposed conference will benefit the academic community of Uttar Pradesh in the following ways:

  • Reduced registration fees for UP-based students and research scholars
  • Opportunities for Ph.D. scholars and young researchers to present their work
  • Interaction with eminent academicians and industry experts
  • Skill enhancement through technical sessions and invited talks
  • Institutional networking and collaborative research opportunities

Expected Outcomes

  • Dissemination of high-quality, peer-reviewed research
  • Improvement in research writing and presentation skills
  • Enhanced academic confidence among UG, PG, and Ph.D. students
  • Awareness of emerging technologies and research directions
  • Promotion of interdisciplinary and applied research
  • Potential research collaborations and funded project id

Scope of the Conference

ICRAME 2026 welcomes original contributions across (but not limited to) the following domains:

  • Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Technologies
  • Electronics, Communication, and Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical, Industrial, and Civil Engineering
  • Interdisciplinary and Emerging Technologies
  • Smart and Sustainable Agriculture Technologies
  • Health Technologies and Bioinformatics
  • Humanities, Social Sciences, and Digital Society
  • Engineering Education, Research, and Innovation

Why Attend?

  • Global Networking: Connect with peers, researchers, industry experts, and thought leaders across diverse domains.
  • Publication Opportunities: Publish in reputed conference proceedings, edited books, and journals indexed in Scopus and other databases with DOI.
  • Workshops & Panels: Gain insights from hands-on workshops, expert panels, and special sessions on emerging technologies.
  • Innovation Showcase: Discover cutting-edge research, prototypes, and real-world technology solutions.
